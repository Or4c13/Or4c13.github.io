∏i=1nciai=m∑i=1nai∗si≡1 mod N\displaystyle \prod_{i=1}^{n}c_{i}^{a_i} = m^{\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i*s_i} \equiv 1\bmod N i=1∏n​ciai​​=m∑i=1n​ai​∗si​≡1modN
